Breakfast

Indulge in a hearty Filipino breakfast at Kasama, where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee mingles with the scent of pandesal baking in the oven. Start your day with our signature silog plates, featuring garlic fried rice, sunny-side-up eggs, and your choice of savory meats such as tocino, longganisa, or tapa. Whether you prefer a traditional champorado or a modern twist on the classic with our ube waffles, our breakfast offerings are sure to energize you for the day ahead.

Lunch

Experience the flavors of the Philippines during lunchtime at Kasama, where our menu boasts an enticing array of dishes that are perfect for a midday feast. Savor the bold flavors of our sinigang, a tangy tamarind soup brimming with tender pork belly and fresh vegetables, or indulge in the comforting warmth of our chicken adobo served with steamed jasmine rice. For a lighter option, our refreshing ensaladas feature crisp greens, tropical fruits, and zesty dressings, providing a perfect balance of flavors to tantalize your taste buds.

Dinner

Start your evening with our selection of appetizers, including crispy lumpia filled with savory pork and vegetables, or our sizzling sisig served on a hot skillet with a squeeze of calamansi. For the main course, delight in our seafood specialties such as grilled bangus or shrimp in coconut milk curry, or indulge in our tender beef kare-kare simmered in a rich peanut sauce. At Kasama, every dinner is a celebration of flavor, culture, and community.

Founded by chefs Genie Kwon and Timothy Flores, Kasama is a culinary destination where traditional Filipino flavors meet contemporary innovation. Our passion for showcasing the richness of Filipino cuisine has earned us the prestigious honor of a Michelin star, a testament to our dedication to excellence in both taste and hospitality.

Together we invite you to embark on a culinary journey that celebrates the vibrant tapestry of Filipino flavors. From our meticulously crafted pastries to our inventive interpretations of classic dishes, each bite is a tribute to the diverse culinary heritage of the Philippines. Our commitment to using locally-sourced, fresh ingredients ensures that every dish is bursting with authenticity and flavor.
